About us

Many years ago, the human race faced the most gruesome torment from the deadly Shinokage (death's shadow). Time and time again, he unleashed his fearless Shinokijins (death soldiers) on earth, wreaking havoc with reckless abandon.

Humans prayed and yearned for a savior until the rise of the Azuki. The Azuki tribe possesses the Katana-Kusanagi, a mystical sword of power capable of killing millions of Shinokijins in a single swerve. The sight of this mystic weapon leaves the Shinokijins trembling, fleeing to their master with fear.

Humans were living fine again due to the Azuki's intervention until the Katana-Kusanagi mysteriously vanished. Shinokage saw it as an avenue to strike again, but this time his target was the Azuki tribe, which brought him a bad reputation as a failure in the underworld!

Without the sword, there was no saving grace for the Azuki and humans. Hence, the Azuki's turned to Shirokinja â€” Shirokage's younger brother and greatest rival. The terms of his support were for the Azuki to forever serve him. The Azuki saw it as a better option than total destruction, believing that one day the human race would be strong enough to help them gain freedom.

Shirokinja delivered his promise of stopping Shinokage. He joined forces with the Azuki to wipe him and his army out entirely. With the whole power and control now with him, he turned the Azuki into faceless skull spirits, and they've been serving him for thousands of years.

It's now time for the human race to repay the Azuki's favor by saving them from Shirokinja slavery. Every skull azuki you own from this collection frees an azuki. It's the least to do for humans' greatest defenders.
